JOM Jaring's Login Help

How do you login?

Steps:
  1. Visit JOM Jaring's Login page
  2. Choose your login preference, either Gmail or Facebook.
  3. Within Facebook or Gmail account, login or registered a service with them.
  4. Allow
    JOM Jaring to authenticate your Facebook/Gmail account.
    NOTE:
    • Check that the address URL is from Google or Facebook.
    • Check the requested permission is from/to JOM Jaring.
    • JOM Jaring is only requesting for grant on your basic personal profile. Not your contacts.
  5. Done!

What is an External Login?

We are not, nor planning, to maintain an internal login for JOM Jaring. JOM Jaring will be using your Gmail and Facebook account as the authentication and identification platform. We will not be taking any advantages from your Gmail/Facebook Account details. If you are in doubt and requires in-depth details, please email and contact us via jomjaring@gmail.com.

Information Gathered

We are only interested to verify your validity as an Internet user. JOM Jaring will and do respect you privacy; hence, we will not reveal any of your information to any third parties without your consensus. We will ONLY keep the below information as references:
  • Your name
  • Your gender
  • Your email
  • Your facebook or gmail identity number to JOM Jaring
